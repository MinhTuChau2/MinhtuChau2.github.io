Project of integration in CEGEP

In this project, we built an alarm system that alerts the user that someone entered a room. To accomplish this, research on lasers, photo-resistors, transistors, magnets and circuits needed to be done.

Here is the simplified circuit in the box. In fact, we added a switch composed of a magnet that activates the alarm system. In fact, if the magnet is not placed properly, the circuit is cut and the alarm system doesn't work.

Digital system project

In this project, the job demanded for this project was to build a digital system from scratch. This digital system had to complete the task of counting the amount of people in a room. Also, the room has a max occupancy of 63 people. When that limit is reached, the room emits a red light. The system needs a synchronous reset system to reset the count of people to zero.
